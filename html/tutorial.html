Tutorial

The images of this tutorial are currently appraised as supplementary materials of a paper describing the metaConvert tools. They will be available as soon as a (positive) decision on this paper is reached.

Format your dataset. To enable the automated calculation of effect sizes, you must pass to the metaConvert tools a well formatted-dataset.
Load your dataset. Once you have formatted your dataset, load it in one of the metaConvert tools. If you are conducting a systematic review, you can benefit from a feature flagging differences between two datasets (e.g., yours and the one of a second data extractor).
Choose appropriate options. Once you have loaded your dataset, choose the different options proposed by metaConvert. Note that the default options are generally those recommended to be used.
Run calculations. No worries, everything is automatic, our functions are working for you!
Interpret the results. In the output, your effect sizes (along with other information) will be displayed. You can retrieve guidance in the output here.
