Direction controls reverse_* Adds a TRUE/FALSE column per statistic so you can flip a study whose outcome runs the opposite way — keeping every effect size pointing the same direction.
Example. Say you define your effect so higher = improvement. Most studies report it that way — leave their reverse_* blank. But one trial scored the scale so higher = worse: put TRUE in that row's reverse_md (or whichever statistic you entered) and metaConvert flips its sign, so it pools in the same direction as the rest.
